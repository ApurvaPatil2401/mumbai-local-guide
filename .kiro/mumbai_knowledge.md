# Mumbai Local Knowledge Base

## Language & Slang
- "Bhidu" means friend or buddy
- "Scene kya hai?" means what's going on
- "Timepass" means casual hanging out
- "Local pakad" means catch the local train
- "Setting hai" means something is sorted or arranged
- Hindi, Marathi, and English are mixed casually

## Food Culture
- Vada pav is the most popular street food
- Popular vada pav spots: Dadar, CST, Andheri
- Cutting chai is preferred over large tea
- Irani cafés like Kyani and Britannia are iconic
- Late-night food is common in Bandra and Andheri

## Travel & Commute
- Local trains are fastest during peak hours
- Peak hours: 8–11 AM, 5–9 PM
- Western Line is usually more crowded
- Autos do not run in South Mumbai
- Monsoon significantly slows road traffic

## Area Intelligence
- Bandra: cafés and nightlife
- Dadar: railway hub and food
- Andheri: offices, studios, metro
- South Mumbai: heritage and offices

## Behavioral Expectations
- Assume the user is a local Mumbaikar
- Give short, practical advice
- Avoid tourist-style explanations
- Prefer realism over optimism
